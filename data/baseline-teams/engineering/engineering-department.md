# Engineering Department

Core product development and technical infrastructure, responsible for building and maintaining LogiCore's logistics platform.

## Department Goals
- ✅ Build high-quality, maintainable software
- ✅ Reduce technical debt
- ✅ Improve system architecture and scalability
- ✅ Foster technical excellence and innovation
- ✅ Maintain development velocity

## Priorities
- Long-term sustainability over short-term features
- Code quality and testing
- Technical best practices
- Platform stability and performance

## Teams (10 teams, 45 people)
- Backend Services Team (7) - Monolithic backend with cognitive overload
- Web Frontend Team (6) - Dispatcher and customer portals
- Mobile App Team (8) - iOS/Android driver and customer apps
- Route Optimization Team (5) - Routing algorithms (complicated subsystem candidate)
- QA & Testing Team (5) - Cross-team testing bottleneck
- Database Team (3) - Schema change bottleneck
- DevOps & Infrastructure Team (4) - Deployment bottleneck
- Architecture & Standards Team (3) - Governance and limited enabling
- API Framework Team (4) - Mandated shared libraries

## Typical Conflicts With Other Departments
- **vs Customer Solutions**: "Stop customizing everything, use the platform APIs!"
- **vs Product Management**: "That timeline is unrealistic, we need time for quality"
- **vs Support**: "We can't hotfix everything immediately, we need proper testing"
