# Engineering Department

Core product development and technical infrastructure, responsible for building and maintaining the RouteOptix platform and supporting technical services.

## Department Goals
- ✅ Build high-quality, maintainable software
- ✅ Reduce technical debt
- ✅ Improve system architecture and scalability
- ✅ Foster technical excellence and innovation
- ✅ Maintain development velocity

## Priorities
- Long-term sustainability over short-term features
- Code quality and testing
- Technical best practices
- Platform stability and performance
