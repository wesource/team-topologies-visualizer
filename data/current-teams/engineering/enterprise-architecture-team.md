# Enterprise Architecture Team

Responsible for architectural governance, technical strategy, and cross-team architectural decisions at the enterprise level.

## Responsibilities
- Define and maintain enterprise architecture standards
- Technical strategy and roadmap planning
- Cross-team architectural decision making
- Architecture reviews and governance
- Technology evaluation and selection
- Technical debt management strategy
- Integration patterns and best practices
- Security architecture and standards
- Performance and scalability guidelines
- Mentoring and guiding development teams
- Proof of concepts for new technologies

## Technologies
- **Architecture Tools**: ArchiMate, C4 Model, Enterprise Architect
- **Documentation**: Confluence, Markdown, Draw.io
- **Analysis**: Various (depends on evaluation needs)
- **Monitoring**: Application performance monitoring tools

## Team Structure
- 1 Chief Architect / Enterprise Architect
- 1 Solution Architect
- 1 Technical Architect

## Key Focus Areas
- Monolith evolution and potential decomposition strategies
- Cloud migration planning
- Microservices transition roadmap
- API strategy and design patterns
- Data architecture and governance
- Security and compliance standards
- Technical skill development initiatives
