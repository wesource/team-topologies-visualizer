# ML Product Team

Specialized team focused on machine learning features for RouteOptix, including delivery time prediction, traffic forecasting, and route optimization through predictive analytics.

## Responsibilities
- Delivery time prediction models (ETA estimation)
- Traffic pattern forecasting and route optimization
- Demand forecasting for delivery capacity planning
- ML model development and deployment
- Feature engineering from historical delivery data
- Data pipeline development for real-time analytics
- A/B testing of optimization algorithms
- Model monitoring and performance optimization
- Business intelligence reporting on delivery performance

## Technologies
- **ML/Data**: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn
- **Data Processing**: Pandas, Spark, Jupyter
- **ML Ops**: MLflow, Docker
- **Database**: Oracle (via read replicas)
- **Visualization**: Tableau, Matplotlib

## Team Structure
- 2 ML Engineers
- 1 Data Engineer
- 1 Analytics Engineer
